# HIGH PRIORITY QUESTIONS (Top 7 for Budget-Conscious Pilot)

How does the speaker describe their approach to practicing presence with clients or communities?
What does the speaker say about sustaining their practice when emotions or circumstances shift?
What specific methods or techniques does the speaker teach or use in their work?
How does the speaker describe the difference between surface-level and deep engagement in their work?
What does the speaker say about boundaries, limitations, or what they don't do in their practice?
What does the speaker say distinguishes their approach from more conventional or mainstream practices?
What traditions, frameworks, or disciplines outside their primary field inform the speaker's work?

---

## Why These 7?

These extract the core information organizations need to discern practitioner fit:

1. **How they practice presence** - depth vs. surface
2. **Sustaining practice** - consistency vs. emotion-dependent  
3. **Specific methods** - concrete, evaluable techniques
4. **Surface vs. deep** - reinforces #1, quality of engagement
5. **Boundaries** - realistic expectations, prevents burnout
6. **What distinguishes them** - unique value proposition
7. **Cross-disciplinary** - holistic/integrative approaches

These map to the most important database fields:
- `how_i_practice` (questions 1, 2, 4, 6)
- `specific_methods` (question 3)
- `boundaries_statement` (question 5)
- `disciplines_and_frameworks` (question 7)
