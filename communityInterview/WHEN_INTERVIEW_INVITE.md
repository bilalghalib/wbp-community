### 3. **Gratitude Loop System**
You use a resource (coach, research, program) ‚Üí System reminds you 2 weeks later ‚Üí You thank them + share what worked ‚Üí Your feedback helps next org.

**Question for you**: Does this feel helpful or annoying? How would you want to be reminded?

### 4. **Research Repository (with Indigenous Protocols)**

- Can't download until you acknowledge protocol
