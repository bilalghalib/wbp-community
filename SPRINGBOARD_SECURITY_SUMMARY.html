How We Keep You Safe

A simple guide to privacy and security on the Springboard platform.

1. Our Core Promise

Your trust is our most valuable asset. We believe that for you to be truly open about your wellbeing, you need to know exactly where your data goes—and where it doesn't.

We Do ✅

  • Protect your privacy and dignity at all times.
  • Explain exactly what we collect and why.
  • Use data only to help the community grow.

We Do Not ❌

  • Collect information we don't need.
  • Track your individual clicks or searches.
  • Sell your data or share it with outsiders.

2. Surveys: The "Blender" Approach

Think of our survey system like a blender. We take answers from many people and mix them together. We only look at the "smoothie" (the trends), never the individual ingredients (you).

Strict Anonymity: We only create reports if there are at least 7 people in the group. If the group is smaller, the report is locked to protect your identity.

Separation: Your email and login info are kept in a separate digital building from your survey answers. There is no technical key to link them back together.

4. Even Admins Have Limits

The people who run the platform (TWP Admins) are there to keep things running, not to watch you.

Admins Can:

  • Create surveys
  • Delete old accounts
  • See global totals

Admins CANNOT:

  • View individual survey responses.
  • Track specific user behavior on the platform.
  • View private data beyond basic account info (email).
